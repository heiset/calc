Syllabus

Expectations and style of class

Functions

week 1: mar 4-8

We review functions and algebra as a prelude to a more in-depth algebra review leading towards calculus.

Exponents

week 2: mar 18-22

We learn about things of special interest: Exponents, Parametric Equations

Trigonometry

week 3: mar 25-29

We learn about trigonometry and Euclid... and catch up on the hw y'all didn't do over break!

Polynomials

week 4: apr 1-5

We learn about polynomials, in particular quadratics, and derive a geometric explanation for the quadratic formula

Systems of equations

week 5: apr 8-12

We learn about systems of equations

Introducing calculus

week 6: apr 22-26

1. Thinking about calc with the motivating examples of e^x and Riemann sums, 2. Limits and continuity, deriving limits for well known polynomials for f(x+h) as h->0, 3. Applications of calc through algebraic ideas we already know

Limits->derivatives

week 7: apr 29-may 3

We extend thinking about slope with the idea of slope at a point, or a limit... and discover that it's a derivative.
